Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 8, 2026
This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by Esbro Labs LLC, including Mancala – Stone & Pit and any future apps we release.

1. Who We Are

Esbro Labs LLC ("Esbro Labs," "we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application developer based in Columbus, Ohio. We build simple, focused games and utility apps for iOS and Android. You can reach us at matthew@esbrolabs.com.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our apps. However, certain data may be collected automatically:

Device and Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings
  • App usage patterns (e.g., how often the app is opened, gameplay interactions)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information

Advertising Data (Free Version Only)

The free version of our apps displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. If you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, AdMob may collect and use your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) to serve personalized ads. If you decline, non-personalized ads will be shown instead. Either way, ads may still appear in the free version.

AdMob may independently collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. We do not control, and are not responsible for, data collected directly by Google.

Purchase Data

If you make an In-App Purchase (such as "Remove Ads"), that transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. We only receive confirmation from Apple that the purchase was completed.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve our apps
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • To serve advertisements in the free version of our apps (via Google AdMob)
  • To fulfill In-App Purchases and restore them when requested

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for any purpose beyond what is described in this policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob – advertising platform. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store – app distribution and In-App Purchase processing. Apple Privacy Policy

5. Your Choices and Controls

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

When you first open one of our apps on iOS, you will see Apple's ATT prompt asking whether to allow tracking. You can select "Allow" for personalized ads or "Ask App Not to Track" for non-personalized ads. You can change this at any time in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Limit Ad Tracking

You can reset or limit your Advertising Identifier at any time in your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

Remove Ads

You can eliminate all advertising entirely by purchasing the "Remove Ads" In-App Purchase available within the app. This is a one-time, permanent purchase. After purchase, no advertising SDKs will be initialized when you use the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are rated 4+ on the App Store and are appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our apps do not require registration, do not include social features, and do not allow users to submit any personal information.

Advertising in our free apps is served through Google AdMob. If you are a parent or guardian with concerns about advertising shown to a child, you may purchase the "Remove Ads" option to disable all ads, or contact us at matthew@esbrolabs.com.

7. Data Retention

We do not maintain servers or databases storing your personal data. Crash and diagnostic data collected through Apple's tools is retained in accordance with Apple's policies. AdMob data retention is governed by Google's policies.

8. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the limited information associated with your use of our apps. Because we do not collect or store personal data on our own servers, the primary security considerations relate to data handled by Apple and Google under their respective policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our apps after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
